The Best Rolled Sugar Cookie

Whenever you make these cookies for someone, be sure to bring along several copies of the recipe! You will be asked for it, I promise! NOTE: I make icing with confectioners' sugar and milk. I make it fairly thin, as I 'paint' the icing on the cookies with a pastry brush. Thin enough to spread easily but not so thin that it just makes your cookies wet and runs off.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup Butter

  • 2 Cups White Sugar

  • 4 eggs

  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract

  • 5 Cups All-Purpose Flour

  • 2 Teaspoons Baking Powder

  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

Steps:

  1. Cream together butter and sugar until until smooth. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir flour, baking powder, and salt. Cover, and chill dough for at least one hour (or overnight).

  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees) C. Roll out dough on floured surface 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick. Cut into shapes with any cookie cutter. Place cookies 1 inch apart on ungreased cookie sheats.

  3. Bake 6 to 8 minutes in preheated oven. Cool completely.
